Institutional Profile

Discover the heritage, leadership, and strategic vision of Rift Valley National Polytechnic.

Legacy of Excellence

Established in 1979 through a community-based harambee initiative, the Rift Valley Institute of Science and Technology (RVIST) was founded to equip Kenyan youth with hands-on technical skills. Under the leadership of President Daniel Toroitich arap Moi, the institution has grown from a regional institute serving 200 students to a National Polytechnic serving over 12,000 students today.

"Training for Self-Reliance"

The founding core objective remains the pillar of our mission.
